Dybbøl Mill
===========

:date: 2015-06-25
:category: Panoramas
:tags: Mühle
:scene: dybbol

Originally build in 1744 the `Dybbøl Mill`_ was destroyed 
and rebuild several times. It was in operation 
till 1990 and used as museum since 1995 (from `Wikipedia`_)
